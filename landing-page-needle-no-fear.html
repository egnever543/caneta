What nobody tells you

The medication fixes your appetite. It doesn't fix everything else.

Most GLP-1 users figure out pretty fast that eating less is the easy part — it's everything that comes after that gets complicated.

Risk 1

Muscle loss

Without adjusting what you eat, a significant portion of the weight you lose may come from muscle — not fat. That slows your metabolism long after the scale moves.

Risk 2

Side effects with no playbook

Nausea, constipation, reflux, low energy. The prescription insert warns you they exist — but rarely tells you what to actually do about them day to day.

Risk 3

The rebound, all over again

When treatment ends and appetite comes back, without solid habits in place, the weight usually follows — and so does the frustration.
